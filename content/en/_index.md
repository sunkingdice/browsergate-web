Microsoft Corporation's Linkedin – the world's largest professional network, by their own admission – is running a massive, global, invasive and potentially illegal spying operation on every computer that visits their website.

#### Executive Summary
in 2024 Microsoft was designated as a so called "gatekeeper technology" under the Digital Markets Act in the EU. The two regulated products are Microsoft Windows and Microsoft Linkedin.

The Digital Markets Act mandates that gatekeepers allow business users and authorized third parties (aka "Tools") free, effective, high-quality, continuous and real-time access to all data (including personal data) that is generated through the use of Linkedin.

This regulation effectively legizes the use of 3rd party tools to access your data on Linkedin and renders section 8.2.2. of LinkedIn Terms and Services void.

Instead of complying with this regulation, Microsoft decided to distract EU regulators in Brussels with what can only be described as compliance theater, while it went on an unhinged "killing spree" of companies who offer Linkedin tools.

As part of their "enforcement" they started to inject malicious software into the browsers of their users - without their knowledge or their consent.

At the time of writing this malicious script downloads a list of 5433 software products and then brute-forces the detection of these software products.

#### A Massive Data Breach of Sensitive Data and Trade Secrets

The malicious javascript program that Microsoft secretly injects in the LinkedIn website, penetrates each users computer and searches for installed software applications. 

The search includes 

- political, 
- religious and 
- health data profiles 

as well as trade secrets of millions of companies that use products from Microsoft competitors.
